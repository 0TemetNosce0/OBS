Settings

  • General: Change or add profiles and general settings.
  • Encoding: Change bitrate, quality, cbr/vbr, etc.
  • Broadcast: Change the broadcast settings and switch between File Output and Live Stream Mode.
  • Video: Change the resolution, downscale, fps and filtering options.
  • Audio: Change the Audio devices etc.
  • Hotkeys: Configure, change or clear the hotkeys
  • Advanced: Change Advanced settings (only recommended if you know the meaning of each settings)
  • Microphone Noise Gate: Add voice activation to your Microphone
