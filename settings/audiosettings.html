--- title: Audio Settings layout: default ---

Audio

Desktop/Microphone/Auxillary Audio Device

Select your desired Input devices for Desktop and Microphone Input. Both devices can be controlled with the Volume Sliders on the Main Window.

Force Microphone/Auxillary to Mono

Self explanatory.

Show only connected devices

Self explanatory.

Use Push-to-Talk

Activate the Push-to-Talk Hotkeys to be used with your Microphone/Auxillary Device. You can set two different Hotkeys, or clear them.

Push-to-talk Delay (milliseconds)

Activate a delay when releasing the PTT button.

Mute/Unmute Mic / Desktop Hotkey

Set Hotkeys to mute your Desktop or Microphone Input.

Desktop / Mic/Auxillary Boost (multiple)

Boost your Desktop and or Mic/Auxillary Input to a higher volume. 1 is the standard value.

Mic Time Offset (milliseconds)

Delay the Microphone with the set amount of milliseconds.
